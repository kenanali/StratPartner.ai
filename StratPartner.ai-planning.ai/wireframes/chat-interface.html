Journey Map: Enterprise Onboarding

stratpartner.ai · March 28, 2025

Stage 1 — Contract Signed → Welcome

Touchpoint What happens Emotional state Pain points
Countersign email DocuSign completion email arrives 😌 Relieved, cautiously optimistic No clear "what happens next" in the email
CSM intro email CSM introduces themselves 48–72 hours later 😐 Neutral — waiting Delay creates anxiety. Buyer wonders if they made the right choice.
Kickoff scheduling Calendly link sent, 30-min kickoff booked 🙂 Engaged but uncertain Calendar link feels transactional. No agenda or expectation setting.

Stage 2 — Kickoff → Admin Setup

  • Moment of truth: The kickoff call. If the CSM shows up knowing the client's context, trust jumps. If they ask "can you tell me about your business?" — it drops immediately.
  • Admin provisioning is where most large accounts stall. Requires IT involvement, SSO configuration, and permission mapping across 5 country orgs.

Key Opportunities

  • Send a "Your onboarding plan" document within 24 hours of contract — show them the roadmap
  • Build a CSM pre-brief template that pulls from CRM data so every kickoff starts with context
  • Create a dedicated multi-country admin playbook
⚡ What I now know
The enterprise onboarding is likely where churn risk is highest — the 48–72 hour gap post-signature is a vulnerability. The multi-country admin problem is probably causing 2–3 week delays on average.

Suggested next step: Run /scan-blockers on the provisioning workflow to identify what's causing the delay before recommending a fix.
